# DeArt

DeArt is an independent visual arts platform focused on documenting and
narrating the contemporary art ecosystem of the Middle East.

## Vision

DeArt explores the relationships between people, spaces, artworks, and
cultural movements through research, visual storytelling, and digital
media.

## Main Sections

### 🎨 Art Professionals

Featuring artists, curators, collectors, gallerists, publishers, and
other key figures shaping the visual arts ecosystem.

### 🏛️ Art Spaces

Exploring galleries, museums, studios, and alternative spaces where art
is created and experienced.

### 🖼️ Artworks

Analyzing important artworks, artistic movements, and visual narratives.

### 💫 Art Events

Covering exhibitions, biennials, cultural gatherings, and art-related
events.

### ♟️ Art Auctions

A strategic look into the art market through the relationship between
professionals, spaces, and artworks.

### 🌍 Residency

Sharing global residency opportunities and open calls for artists.

## Current Regional Focus

-   Turkey
-   Egypt
-   Lebanon

## Mission

To build a bridge between research, visual storytelling, and
contemporary Middle Eastern art.
